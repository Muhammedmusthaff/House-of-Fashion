FAQ

1.What should men wear with trousers?

   Trousers for men are very versatile and can be paired with any solid-colored shirts for men to make a stylish look. When pairing patterned trousers for men try not to select shirts with checks or stripes, patterns over patterns crowd the look creating a cluster of designs that do not go well together.

2.How many pairs of trousers should a man have?

   To start with you should have at least one pair of trousers for every day of the week. When buying your Allen Solly trousers for men try and have at least four of the seven be a solid color like navy blue trousers for men, black trousers for men, or grey trousers for men. The rest can be stripped or checked. We offer a plethora of trouser and chino options to choose from when it comes time to expand your wardrobe.

3.How do I choose the right chinos?

   We have a variety of fit options for chinos for men, you'd want to pick a fit that best accentuates your body type. Chinos are designed to fit snug, giving you a neat n-clean high fashion look. Once you have a pattern or color you like, look for a size that fits comfortable around your waist. Also, look out for the length, you don't want your chinos for men dragging on the floor while you walk. Chinos for men are designed to fit right by the ankle and no lower.

4.What color chinos are the best?

   For your Chinos try and stick with solid colors, they will pair easily with other shirts and t-shirts for men in your wardrobe. We have designed chinos for men with versatility and comfort in mind, using colors that go well with any look. When buying Allen Solly Chinos pick colors like dark blues, beiges, and greys, maybe some pastels if you're feeling a bit more adventurous.

5.What are some outfit ideas for wearing navy blue trousers for different occasions?

   Men's Navy blue trousers offer a versatile and timeless option that can be styled for various occasions. For a formal or professional setting, pair navy blue trousers with a crisp white dress shirt and a blazer for a sharp and sophisticated look. Complete the outfit with dress shoes or loafers. For a smart-casual occasion, navy blue trousers can be paired with a patterned or colored button-down shirt and a knit sweater or cardigan. Finish the look with suede shoes or leather boots. For a more relaxed and casual setting, navy blue trousers can be worn with a simple t-shirt or polo shirt and sneakers or boat shoes. Navy blue trousers provide a versatile base to create different outfits suitable for both formal and casual events.
